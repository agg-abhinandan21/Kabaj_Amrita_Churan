Causes of Constipation

Common lifestyle and health-related reasons that lead to constipation.

Low Fiber Diet

Eating less fruits, vegetables, and whole grains reduces fiber intake, making stools harder.

Lack of Exercise

Physical inactivity slows down digestion and bowel movement.

Change in Routine

Travel, stress, or irregular lifestyle can disturb your digestive rhythm.

Excess Medication

Overuse of certain medicines like painkillers and antacids may cause constipation.
